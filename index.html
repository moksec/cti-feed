A 25-minute BGP route leak incident affected IPv6 traffic, caused by an accidental policy misconfiguration on a Cloudflare router in Miami. The misconfiguration led to an overly permissive export policy, causing Cloudflare to advertise internal IPv6 routes externally to BGP neighbors, resulting in Type 3 and Type 4 route leaks as defined by RFC7908.
