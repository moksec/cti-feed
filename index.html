DKnife is an ELF framework comprised of seven Linux-based components, functioning as a post-compromise AitM toolkit on compromised edge devices (routers). Its modules include `dknife.bin`, `postapi.bin`, `sslmm.bin` (a HAProxy-derived reverse proxy), `yitiji.bin` (which creates a bridged TAP interface at 10.3.3.3 for traffic manipulation), `remote.bin` (an n2n VPN client), `mmdown.bin` (an Android APK downloader/updater), and `dkupdate.bin`.
